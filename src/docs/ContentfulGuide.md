
# Staydia Sports - Contentful CMS Guide for Communications Team

This guide will help you manage news articles on the Staydia Sports website using Contentful, our content management system.

## Accessing Contentful

1. Go to [Contentful](https://app.contentful.com/) and log in with your credentials
2. Select the "Staydia Sports" space
3. Click on "Content" in the top navigation

## Creating a New Article

1. Click the "Add entry" button and select "News Article"
2. Fill in the following fields:
   - **Title**: The headline of your article
   - **Slug**: URL-friendly version of the title (lowercase, hyphens instead of spaces)
   - **Category**: Type of article (e.g., "NEWS", "ANNOUNCEMENT", "FEATURE")
   - **Date**: Publication date
   - **Summary**: Brief introduction or excerpt (1-3 sentences)
   - **Featured Image**: Upload or select an image for the article
   - **Body Content**: Full article content using the rich text editor

### Tips for Images
- Recommended size: 1200×630 pixels
- Keep file size under 2MB for fast loading
- Use descriptive file names
- Add alternative text to images for accessibility

### Tips for Writing Content
- Use clear, concise headlines
- Structure content with headings (H2, H3)
- Keep paragraphs short and focused
- Use bullet points for lists
- Include quotes and statistics when relevant

## Publishing Your Article

1. After filling in all fields, click "Save" in the top-right corner
2. Review your article in the preview
3. When ready, click "Publish" to make it live on the website
4. Your article will appear in the Newsroom section on the homepage and on the News page

## Editing Existing Articles

1. Navigate to "Content" and find your article in the list
2. Click on the article to open it
3. Make your changes
4. Click "Save" and then "Publish" to update the live version
